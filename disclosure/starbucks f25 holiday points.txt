1 Limited-Time Promotional Offer (“Bonus Offer”) starts on November 25, 2024, at 22:30:00 EST and ends December 22, 2024, at 02:59:00 EST (the “Offer Period”) and is only available to  those account holders of a Canadian Starbucks Rewards account (“Starbucks Rewards Account”) who are also a primary or additional cardholder (each, an “Eligible Cardholder” or “you”) of an account for any of the following products issued by The Toronto-Dominion Bank (“TD”) that are open, active and in good standing: TD Rewards Visa* Card, TD Platinum Travel Visa* Card, TD First Class Travel® Visa Infinite* Card, and TD Business Travel Visa* Card (each, an “Eligible TD Card Account”).  

Redeem and Earn Bonus
To earn 200 bonus Starbucks Stars (“Bonus Stars”), an Eligible Cardholder must redeem, in a single or multiple transactions, a minimum of 700 TD Rewards Points for Starbucks Stars in My TD Rewards during the Offer Period.  You will not be eligible to earn, and you will not be awarded, these Bonus Stars if: (i) you have not linked your Starbucks Rewards Account with your Eligible TD Card Account; (ii) you have previously redeemed TD Rewards Points for Starbucks Stars; or (iii) you have previously received bonus Stars in connection with redeeming TD Rewards Points for Starbucks Stars. For greater certainty, you can only earn a maximum of 200 Bonus Stars pursuant to this Bonus Offer, even if you redeem 700 or more TD Rewards Points for Starbucks Stars in My TD Rewards more than once during the Offer Period. 

ADDITIONAL CONDITIONS
To be eligible to earn Bonus Stars under this Bonus Offer, your Eligible TD Card Account must be open and in good standing during the entire Offer Period. Bonus Stars available under this Bonus Offer will only be awarded one time per Eligible Cardholder. Bonus Stars earned pursuant to this Bonus Offer will typically be deposited by Starbucks to the applicable Eligible Cardholder’s Starbucks Account within 1 to 2 business days of the date the account linking is completed so long as such Starbucks Account is open and active at the time the Bonus Stars are to be deposited. Bonus Stars will not be applied and will be irrevocably forfeited if your Starbucks Rewards Account is not open and in good standing at the time the Bonus Stars are to be applied by Starbucks to your Starbucks Rewards Account.
