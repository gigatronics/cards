More Card Benefits and Features

Your TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Privilege Card comes with special Air Canada travel benefits 4 . Relax before takeoff with access to a Maple Leaf LoungeTM. 5 Primary Cardholders and Additional Cardholders on the account can access Air Canada Maple Leaf Lounges in North America whether they’re flying together or separately with a same day ticket on a departing Air Canada or Star Alliance® flight. They can even each bring a guest.

Travel exceptionally with someone you love with the Annual Worldwide Companion Pass. As the Primary Cardholder, you can take advantage of an annual round-trip companion pass, which entitles you to buy a companion ticket from $99 (plus taxes, fees, charges and surcharges). You get a companion pass automatically on your Card anniversary if you have spent $25,000 on net purchases 25.

Whether you book using points or cash, as the Primary Cardholder or as an Additional Cardholder 4 , you and travel companions (up to eight) travelling on the same reservation will enjoy the following travel benefits on any flight operated by Air Canada, Air Canada Rouge® or under the Air Canada Express® banner:

  • Priority Check-In. Check-in faster at the airport6. You and up to eight travel companions travelling on the same reservation will all receive priority check-in for any trip that originates on an Air Canada flight.
  • Priority Boarding. Get on board your flight earlier6. You and up to eight travel companions travelling on the same reservation will all enjoy Zone 2 priority boarding for any Air Canada flight.
  • Priority Baggage Handling. Save time leaving the airport6. All your baggage will be tagged priority, so you and up to eight travel companions travelling on the same reservation will find your luggage among the first on the carousel when arriving at your destination. This applies to travel that originates on an Air Canada flight.
  • Priority Airport Standby. Get better access to seats when you’re on standby7. If you and travel companions (up to eight) travelling on the same reservation are on standby at the airport for an Air Canada flight, get on your way with priority. Should a seat become available, you will be assigned it before members who have the same status but don’t hold a TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Privilege Card.
  • Priority Airport Upgrades. Receive higher priority for airport upgrades7. If you’re a Cardholder with Aeroplan Elite TM Status, you and up to eight travel companions travelling on the same reservation get higher priority when you’re on standby for an upgrade at the airport on Air Canada flights. Should a seat become available, you will be assigned to it before members who have the same status but don’t hold a TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Privilege Card.
  • Reach Aeroplan Elite® Status Faster27
    Primary Cardholders can get 1,000 SQC for every $5,000 in eligible net purchases charged to the card account, up to 25,000 SQC27.
  • Get a 10% Head Start28
    Starting in 2027, eligible Primary Cardholders with Aeroplan Elite® status will receive a 10% boost toward qualification, based on Status Qualifying Credits (SQC) accumulated the year before28.
  • Enjoy your eUpgrades longer29. Primary Cardholders who also hold Aeroplan Elite Status will benefit from an extra 12 months to use newly issued eUpgrade credits, for a total of 24 months from issuance.
