More Card Benefits and Features

Whether you book using Points or cash, as the Primary Cardholder or as an Additional Cardholder7, you and travel companions (up to eight) travelling on the same reservation on any flight operated by Air Canada, Air Canada Rouge® or under the Air Canada Express® banner will enjoy:

 

  • Travel lightly through the airport and save on baggage fees3. Primary Cardholders, Additional Cardholders, and travel companions (up to eight) travelling on the same reservation will all enjoy their first checked bag free (up to 23kg/50lbs) when your travel originates on an Air Canada flight.
  • Reach Aeroplan Elite® Status Faster23
    Primary Cardholders can get 1,000 SQC for every $20,000 in eligible net purchases charged to the card account, up to 25,000 SQC23.
  • Complimentary upgrade to Avis® Preferred Plus26: Unlock one-car class rental upgrades, expedited service, dedicated phone line, parking stall and pick-up counter and more by elevating to Avis Preferred Plus.

  • Complimentary Visa Infinite Concierge8: On-call 24 hours a day, seven days a week, the Visa Infinite Concierge can help with any Cardholder request — big or small, to help you get the most out of life whenever you travel, shop and use your Card. Call toll-free from Canada and the U.S.: 1-888-853-4458 From outside Canada and the U.S.: 1-630-350-4545
  • Visa Infinite Luxury Hotel Collection9: Receive seven exclusive benefits when you book your stay through the Visa Infinite Luxury Hotel Collection featuring over 900 of the world’s most intriguing properties.
  • Visa Infinite Dining Series9: As a Visa Infinite Cardholder, you have access to exclusive gourmet events that cater to your astute tastes. The Visa Infinite Dining Series serves up culinary explorations with prized chefs and acclaimed wines.
  • Visa Infinite Wine Country Program9: Show your Visa Infinite card onsite and you can experience exclusive benefits at over 95 participating wineries in British Columbia, Ontario and Sonoma Valley including discounts on wine purchases, complimentary tastings, private vineyard tours and much more.
  • Visa Infinite Entertainment Access9: Get exclusive access to tickets to curated events across Canada throughout the year. Visa Infinite Cardholders can also enjoy exclusive benefits, access and offers for the Toronto International Film Festival®
  • Visa Infinite Troon Golf9: Receive elevated Troon Rewards® Silver Status at over 95 courses and 10% off on green fees, merchandise and lessons at participating Troon Rewards golf courses.

Frequently asked questions

If you are looking for a card that allows you to enjoy exclusive travel benefits and also lets you earn Aeroplan points on everyday purchases then the TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Card could be right for you. You can earn a welcome bonus of 10,000 Aeroplan points when you make your first purchase with your new card1. You can also earn Aeroplan points on all eligible purchases5 and enjoy perks like a free first bag check-in3 when flying with Air Canada. You can redeem your earned Aeroplan points for travel rewards like Air Canada flights, hotels, car rentals and more.

Applying for a TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Credit Card is easy. Click on Apply online on this page and follow the instructions. As part of the application, you will need to share some mandatory information such as your full legal name and date of birth, your home address, phone number and email address, employment status, gross annual income and residential status.

You can earn Aeroplan points on eligible everyday purchases with your TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Credit Card. Earn 1.5 Aeroplan points5 for every dollar spent on this card to purchase eligible gas, electric vehicle charging, grocery or direct through Air Canada® purchases (including Air Canada Vacations®). You can also earn Aeroplan points twice6 when you pay with your Card and provide your Aeroplan number at over 150 Aeroplan partner brands and at 200+ online retailers via the Aeroplan eStore. Your Aeroplan points will not expire as long as you are a primary cardholder in good standing27.

It's easy to redeem your Aeroplan points. You can choose to redeem your points when booking flights with Air Canada or other travel bookings like hotels or car rentals through the Air Canada website or the Air Canada mobile app. You can also redeem your Aeroplan points for premium merchandise, gift cards, activities and much more at the Aeroplan eStore.
