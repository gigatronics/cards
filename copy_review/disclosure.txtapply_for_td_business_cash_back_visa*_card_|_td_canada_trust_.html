TD Business Cash Back Visa* Card

Invest back into your business with Cash Back Dollars.
  • Annual Fee $0
  • Interest Purchases 19.99%
  • Interest Cash Advances 22.99%
  • Additional Cards1 $0
Secure Open an account
Eligibility requirements: You are a Canadian resident and are of the age of majority in your province/territory of residence.

It’s easy to earn Cash Back Dollars

  • Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars

    Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars on Gas, Electric Vehicle Charging and Public Transit Purchases2.

  • Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars

    Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars on recurring bill payments and Streaming, Digital Gaming & Media3

  • Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars

    Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars on eligible Office Supply Purchases2

  • Earn 0.5% in Cash Back Dollars

    Earn 0.5% in Cash Back Dollars on all other Purchases made with your Card4.

Make Cash Back Dollars a part of your business with the TD Business Cash Back Visa* Card

  • No annual fee.
  • Your Cash Back Dollars never expire as long as your account is open and in good standing6.
  • Shop knowing you have coverage for most of your eligible new purchases with Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection5.
  • Link your TD Visa Business Card to Visa SavingsEdge* for free and turn your spending into savings7.
  • Get access to the Card Management Tool: An easy way to access online reporting, review your business expenses, manage your existing credit limits and apply spend controls.
  • Your Cash Back Dollars are credited back to your business account as an automatic statement credit on an annual basis6.
  • This card is made from 90% recycled plastic.

Earn more at Starbucks

Link your eligible Card to earn 50% more Stars at participating Starbucks stores. Conditions apply.

More card benefits and features

  1. Save with Avis Rent A Car and Budget Rent A Car. Use your TD Credit Card to save a minimum of 10% of the lowest available base rates8,9 in Canada and the U.S., and a minimum of 5% of the lowest available base rates8,9 internationally, on qualifying car rentals at participating Avis and Budget locations.

  1. Visa SavingsEdge* Program7:
    Link your TD Visa Business Card to Visa SavingsEdge for free and turn your spending into savings.
    After linking your Card, you’ll get access to hundreds of Cashback Offers to help you save on business expenses — from gas, restaurants, business services, and more.
    Learn more and link

  • Chip & PIN technology:
    TD Visa* Cards with Chip and PIN technology provide an added level of security through the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN). A PIN makes it much more difficult for unauthorized users to copy or access the information on your TD Credit Card.
  • Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection5:
    You automatically have access to Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection, covering most eligible new items you’ve purchased with your Card, should they be stolen or damaged within 90 days of purchase. If the item comes with a manufacturer’s warranty valid in Canada, you may be entitled to double the warranty period for up to 12 additional months.
  • Visa Secure* - Visa Secure provides you with increased security and convenience when you shop online through In-app Authentication.
  • Instant alerts. Added convenience, with TD Fraud Alerts:
    You can now automatically receive TD Fraud Alerts to your mobile phone any time we suspect unusual activity on your TD Business Cash Back Visa* Card. Simply ensure that we have your current mobile phone number listed in your TD Customer Profile. Learn more.
  • Click to Pay
    Paying online with your TD Visa card is easy, smart and secure. Just click to pay with your TD Visa when you see this icon ( ) where Visa is accepted.

For more information, please refer to Cardholder Agreement.

2 Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars (“Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Rebate”) for each $1.00 in “Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Purchases” charged to your TD Business Cash Back Visa Card Account (“Account”), earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars (“Public Transit Rebate”) for each $1.00 in “Public Transit Purchases” and earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars (“Office Supply Rebate”) for each $1.00 in “Office Supply Purchases” charged to your Account. Any returned items, refunds, rebates or other similar credits will reduce or cancel the Cash Back Dollars earned on the original Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Purchases, Public Transit Purchases, or Office Supply Purchases, as applicable. $15,000 Cap: Each of the Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Rebate, the Public Transit Rebate, and the Office Supply Rebate is only available to a $15,000 maximum annual spend (“$15,000 Cap”). Once the $15,000 Cap has been reached for Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Purchases, Public Transit Purchases, or Office Supply Purchases, then any further Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Purchases, Public Transit Purchases, and Office Supply Purchases made on the Account will earn Cash Back Dollars at the standard rebate of 0.5% that applies to all other Purchases on the Account as set out in your Cardholder Agreement (“Standard Rebate”). Applicability of Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Rebate, Public Transit Rebate, and Office Supply Rebate: Each of the Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Rebate, the Public Transit Rebate, and the Office Supply Rebate is in place of, and not in addition to, the Standard Rebate. Merchant Category Codes: To earn the Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Rebate, the Public Transit Rebate, and/or Office Supply Rebate, the Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Purchases and/or Office Supply Purchases must be made at merchants classified through the Visa network with a Merchant Category Code (“MCC”) that identifies them as Gas Service Stations, Automated Fuel Dispensers, Electric Vehicle Charging, Local and Suburban Commuter Passenger Transportation, including Ferries, or Office Supplies. Some merchants may sell gas or gas station services or products, offer electric vehicle charging, offer transit services, or sell office supply items, or have separate merchants located on their premises that also sell gas or gas station services or products, or electric vehicle charging, or transit services, or office supply items, but may not be classified with a gas, electric vehicle charging, public transit, or office supply MCC and such Purchases will not earn the Gas and Electric Vehicle Charging Rebate, Public Transit Rebate, or Office Supply Rebate, as applicable. If you have questions about the MCC that applies to a Purchase, contact TD at 1-800-983-8472. Other Conditions: Offer may be changed, withdrawn or extended at any time and cannot be combined with any other offer.

3 Earn 2% in Cash Back Dollars (“PAP and Streaming, Digital Gaming & Media Rebate”) on each $1.00 in “PAP and Streaming, Digital Gaming & Media Purchases” and payments made on a monthly or other regularly recurring basis for merchant bills, including insurance premiums, magazine/newspaper/online streaming subscriptions, membership fees and telecommunication fees, that are set up by the merchant with your consent to be automatically charged to your TD Business Cash Back Visa Card Account (“Account”) and that are classified through the Visa network as “Recurring Bill Payments” . You must confirm with your merchant if they can accept Pre-Authorized Payments. Any returned items, refunds, rebates or other similar credits will reduce or cancel the Cash Back Dollars earned on the original Recurring Bill Payment.
