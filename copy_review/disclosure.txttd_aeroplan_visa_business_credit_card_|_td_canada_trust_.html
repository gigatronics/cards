TD® Aeroplan® Visa* Business Card

Eligibility requirements: You are a Canadian resident and are of the age of majority in your province/territory of residence.

Special Offer

Up to $1,676 in value~ including up to 60,000 Aeroplan points could be yours with additional travel benefits. Conditions apply. Account must be approved, open and in Good Standing. Offer effective as of January 13, 2026.

  1. Earn a welcome bonus of 10,000 Aeroplan points when the Primary Cardholder3 make their first purchase with their new cardΓ

  2. Monthly Bonus of 2,500 Aeroplan points per month up to 30,000 Aeroplan points could be yours when the Primary Cardholder1 spends $2,500 in purchases each month for the first 12 months of Account approvalΓ

  3. PAP Bonus up to 20,000 Aeroplan Points earned when you set up new regularly recurring bill paymentsΓ

  4. Apply for the NEXUS program membership and once every 48 months get an application fee rebate (up to $100 CAD)16, share free first checked bags4 and get access to Maple Leaf Lounges9

It’s easy to earn Aeroplan points

  • Earn 2 points

    for every $1 spent on eligible purchases made directly with Air Canada® including Air Canada Vacations®.6

  • Earn 1.5 points

    for every $1 you spend on eligible purchases for travel & transit, dining or electric vehicle charging and select business categories such as shipping, internet, cable and phone services made on your Card.6

  • Earn 1 point

    for every $1 you spend on all other eligible purchases on your Card.6

  • Earn points twice

    When you pay and provide your Aeroplan number at over 150 Aeroplan partner brands and at 200+ online retailers via the Aeroplan eStore.2

Elevate your business – and your business travel

Start Combining Aeroplan points you earn with your TD® Aeroplan® Visa* Business Card and any personal TD® Aeroplan Credit Card.  

  • Flexible ways to redeem Aeroplan points for flights, car rentals, hotels and more.
  • Free first checked bag for up to 9 people travelling on the same reservation on Air Canada flights4.
  • Link your TD Visa Business Card to Visa SavingsEdge* for free and turn your spending into savings7.
  • Earn Aeroplan points on every eligible business purchase.
  • Having the flexibility to use points for part of your trip and use your TD® Aeroplan® Visa Business Card to pay for the rest (and earn points, too).
  • Get access to the TD Card Management Tool. An easy way to access online reporting, review your business expenses, manage your existing credit limits, and apply spend controls.
  • 9 types of insurance coverage including travel medical insurance5, flight/trip delay insurance11 and more. Learn more.
  • This card is made from 90% recycled plastic.

All Additional Cardholders must register to connect their Aeroplan number to your Card in order to receive access to applicable airline benefits8.

Earn more at Starbucks

Link your eligible Card to earn 50% more Stars at participating Starbucks stores. Conditions apply.

More card benefits and features

  • Earn big rewards on the little things.
    Earn 50% more Aeroplan points and 50% more Stars at participating Starbucks® stores. Simply link your TD Aeroplan Business Visa Card to your Starbucks® Rewards account. Conditions apply. Learn more
  • Visa SavingsEdge* program7
    Link your TD Visa Business Card to Visa SavingsEdge for free and turn your spending into savings.
    After linking your Card, you’ll get access to hundreds of Cashback Offers to help you save on business expenses — from gas, restaurants, business services, and more.

You have the flexibility to use points for part of your trip and use your TD® Aeroplan® Visa Business Card to pay for the rest (and earn points, too).

Whether you book using points or cash, enjoy the following benefits on any flight operated by Air Canada®, Air Canada Rouge® or under the Air Canada Express® banner.

  • Free First Checked Bag4
    Travel lightly through the airport and save on baggage fees. The Primary Business Cardholder3, Additional Cardholders, and travel companions (up to eight) travelling on the same reservation will all enjoy their first checked bag free (up to 23kg/50lb) when travel originates on an Air Canada flight.

    All Additional Cardholders must link their Aeroplan number to receive access to eligible Air Canada travel benefits.
  • Maple Leaf Lounge One-Time Guest Passes9
    Relax before take-off, using your Maple Leaf Lounge guest pass. The Primary Business Cardholder will earn a pass for every $10,000 of net purchases (less any returns and credits), up to a maximum of four passes a year. Passes can be used by the Primary Business Cardholder or Additional Cardholders and their guest, at select Maple Leaf Lounges in North America with a same day ticket on a departing Air Canada flight.
  • Reach Aeroplan Elite® Status Faster21
    Primary Business Cardholders3 can get 1,000 SQC for every $20,000 in eligible net purchases charged to the card account, up to 25,000 SQC21.
  • Complimentary upgrade to Avis® Preferred Plus22: Unlock one-car class rental upgrades, expedited service, dedicated phone line, parking stall and pick-up counter and more by elevating to Avis Preferred Plus.

Wherever you're headed on your next trip, travel prepared with:

  • Travel Medical Insurance5:
    Up to $2 million of coverage for the first 15 days. If you or your spouse is aged 65 or older, you have coverage for the first 4 days. Additional top-up coverage is available.
  • Trip Cancellation/Trip Interruption Insurance10:
    For Trip Cancellation, up to $1,500 of coverage per insured person, with a maximum of $5,000 for all insured persons, and for Trip Interruption, up to $5,000 of coverage per insured person, with a maximum of $25,000 for all insured persons on the same covered trip.
  • Flight/Trip Delay Insurance11:
    Up to $500 in coverage per insured person if your flight/trip is delayed for over 4 hours.
  • Common Carrier Travel Accident Insurance12:
    Up to $500,000 of coverage for covered losses while travelling on a common carrier (for example, a bus, ferry, plane, train, or auto rental).
  • Delayed and Lost Baggage Insurance11:
    Up to $1,000 overall coverage toward the purchase of essentials, such as clothes and toiletries, if your baggage is delayed for over 6 hours or lost.
  • Auto Rental Collision/Loss Damage Insurance13:
    Coverage for up to 48 consecutive days.
  • Hotel/Motel Burglary Insurance14:
    Up to $2,500 of coverage per occurrence for eligible personal items stolen from your hotel or motel room that belong to the Cardholder on the Account and eligible family members travelling with the Cardholder.
  • Emergency Travel Assistance Services15:
    Help is just a call away. Toll-free access to help in the event of a personal emergency while travelling.
  • Top-up or Extension of coverage26
    You can purchase extra days of travel medical coverage for a specific trip if you will be away for a longer period of time than what your TD Credit Card covers. Or add an additional coverage amount to your trip cancellation and trip interruption insurance if your trip cost is higher than the coverage offered on your card.  Learn more and get a quote.
  • Save time at the border with NEXUS 16:
    Apply for a NEXUS program membership and once every 48 months get an application fee rebate (up to $100 CAD). Additional Cardholders can also take advantage of this NEXUS program application fee rebate.

Save with Avis Rent A Car and Budget Rent A Car.

Use your TD Credit Card to save a minimum of 10% off the lowest available base rates17,18 in Canada and the U.S., and a minimum of 5% off the lowest available base rates17,18 internationally, on qualifying car rentals at participating Avis and Budget locations.

  • Chip & PIN technology:
    TD Visa Cards with Chip and PIN technology provide an added level of security through the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN). A PIN makes it much more difficult for unauthorized users to copy or access the information on your TD Credit Card.
  • Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection19:
    You automatically have access to Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection, covering eligible new items you’ve purchased with your Card, should they be stolen or damaged within 90 days of purchase. If the item comes with a manufacturer's warranty valid in Canada, you may be entitled to double the warranty period for up to 12 additional months.
  • Visa Secure*:
    Visa Secure provides you with increased security and convenience when you shop online through In-app Authentication.
  • Mobile Device Insurance14:
    Get mobile device protection of up to $1,000 in the event of loss, theft, accidental damage or mechanical breakdown for eligible mobile devices when you use your Card to pay all of your monthly mobile bill payments and any upfront costs, or charge at least 75% of the purchase price when paying the full cost of your new mobile device up front.
  • Instant Alerts with TD Fraud Alerts20:
    You can automatically receive TD Fraud Alerts to your mobile phone any time we suspect suspicious activity on your TD® Aeroplan® Visa Business Card. Simply ensure that we have your current mobile phone number listed in your TD Customer Profile. Learn more.
  • Click to Pay
    Paying online with your TD Visa card is easy, smart and secure. Just click to pay with your TD Visa when you see this icon (  )where Visa is accepted.

Review the Product Summaries (For Quebec Residents Only) for more details about insurance coverages.

  • Use Apple Pay, Google Pay or Samsung Pay wherever contactless payments are accepted - These Digital Wallets are accepted in a growing number of shops, restaurants, and other retailers, making it convenient and easy to use for everyday business payments with just a tap of your device25.
  • Cash Advances :
    You can access a Cash Advance at over one million ATMs worldwide, at all TD branches and at select financial institutions around the world.
