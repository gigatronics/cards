Manage your Business Expenses and Reap TD Rewards Points.

Manage your business expenditures using TD tools while earning TD Rewards Points, whether you use your card for dining purchases, streaming, recurring bill payments, foreign currency bills or booking travel. The rewards will add up quickly so you can enhance your travel experience or enjoy a wide variety of rewards. 

  • Get access to the TD Card Management Tool: An easy way to access online reporting, review your business expenses, manage your existing credit limits and apply spend controls. 
  • Enjoy ExpediaForTD.com, your exclusive travel booking site with special offers designed just for you7  and you can be confident that you’ll earn the most rewards points and get the best point redemption value7.
  • No travel blackouts8, no seat restrictions8 and no expiry for your TD Rewards Points as long as your account is open and in good standing9.
  • Transform your everyday business purchases into TD Rewards Points. Choose how to redeem your points for the rewards you want including ExpediaForTD.com, Amazon.ca, cash credits that can be applied to pay down your TD credit card balance.
  • Link your TD Visa Business Card to Visa SavingsEdge* for free and turn your spending into savings11. 
  • 7 types of insurance coverage including travel medical insurance12, flight/trip delay insurance15 and more. 
  • This card is made from 90% recycled plastic.

Redeem your TD Rewards Points how you want, for what you want 

Every purchase you make with your TD Rewards Credit Card earns you TD Rewards Points. With the flexibility to choose how to redeem your TD Rewards Points for the rewards you want, you can use your points on travel, everyday perks, cash credits and more. Learn more.

More card benefits and features

The TD Rewards Program lets you earn TD Rewards Points on your everyday business purchases and gives you the freedom and flexibility to redeem them how and when you want.

  • Go Places : You can earn more TD Rewards Points when you book your business travel online with your TD Business Travel Visa Card through ExpediaForTD.com. You can also redeem10 any earned TD Rewards Points towards that purchase at time of booking to pay for all or part of your trip.
  • Pay off Purchases: Redeem your points for any of your eligible past purchases on your TD Credit Card in just three clicks. It’s a quick, easy way to get the rewards you want most. Learn More
  • Amazon.ca Shop with Points
    Redeem your TD Rewards Points towards making business purchases at Amazon.ca to get what you want, when you want, quickly and easily. Conditions apply. Learn more about how you can redeem your points for all or just part of your purchase.
  • Help pay down your balance: You can also redeem your TD Rewards Points to help pay down your Credit Card Account balance through EasyWeb or the TD Mobile app.
